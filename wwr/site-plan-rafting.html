Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to efficiently present the various white-water experiences we offer to our customers, answer common questions about those experiences, and then allow our customers to quickly and conveniently book white water expeditions.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

Our target customers are white-water enthusiasts, guided white-water rafting groups, and travel coordinators for business excursions and high adventure vacations

What do they want?

They want to experience the thrill of white-water rafting in guided and safe environment

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They can expect to find all of our white-water routes, services, rates, and booking availabilities
